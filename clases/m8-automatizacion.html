🎯 EXAMEN CCNA 200-301

¿Qué entra en el examen?

El dominio 6.0 Automation and Programmability representa aproximadamente el 10% del examen. Es uno de los temas con mayor crecimiento en importancia en las últimas versiones del examen.

6.1Explain how automation impacts network management
6.2Compare traditional networks with controller-based networking
6.3Describe controller-based, software defined architectures (overlay, underlay, fabric)
6.4Compare traditional campus vs SD-Access solutions
6.5Describe characteristics of REST-based APIs (CRUD, HTTP verbs, data encoding)
6.6Recognize the capabilities of configuration management tools (Puppet, Chef, Ansible)
6.7Recognize components of JSON-encoded data
